IRIS

IRIS (International Railway Industry Standard) is an international standard for the railway industry that provides a framework for quality management systems. It was developed by the International Association of the Public Transport Industry (UITP) and the European Railway Agency (ERA) in collaboration with the railway industry. IRIS is based on ISO 9001:2015 and includes specific requirements for the railway industry. Organizations that meet the requirements of IRIS can be certified to the standard, which demonstrates their commitment to quality and customer satisfaction in the railway industry. IRIS certification is recognized by major railway operators and suppliers, and it is considered as a benchmark for quality in the railway industry.
