FSSC

FSSC 22000 is a global food safety management system certification standard that covers the entire food supply chain from primary production to final consumption. It is based on ISO 22000, ISO/TS 22002-1 and additional FSSC 22000 requirements. Organizations that meet the requirements of FSSC 22000 can be certified to the standard, which demonstrates their commitment to food safety and quality. FSSC 22000 is recognized by the Global Food Safety Initiative (GFSI) and is accepted by major retailers and suppliers worldwide. It covers the requirements for food safety management, including food safety hazards, operational prerequisite programs and HACCP (Hazard Analysis and Critical Control Points) principles. The certification process is carried out by independent third-party certification bodies, that ensure the company is following the standard requirements and implementing the best practices.
