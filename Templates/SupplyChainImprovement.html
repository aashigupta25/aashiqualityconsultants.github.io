AASHI Quality Consultants (AQC) is a company that specializes in helping organizations improve their supply chain management. They offer a wide range of services designed to optimize the performance of the supply chain, and ensure compliance with industry standards. Here are some examples of how AQC can help organizations improve their supply chain:

    Quality Assurance: AQC can help organizations to ensure that their suppliers are providing products and services that meet the required quality standards. This can include conducting supplier audits, reviewing quality records and implementing quality assurance plans.
    Supply Chain Optimization: AQC can work with organizations to optimize their supply chain operations. This can include using operations research models to analyze data and improve inventory management, reduce lead times, and minimize transportation costs.
    Compliance Management: AQC can help organizations to ensure that their suppliers are compliant with regulations and industry standards. This can include reviewing supplier contracts, policies and procedures, and providing guidance on compliance requirements.

Supplier Development: AQC can work with organizations to identify and develop new suppliers, as well as improve the performance of existing suppliers. This can include providing training and mentoring for suppliers, and implementing supplier development programs.

    Risk Management: AQC can help organizations to identify and mitigate risks in their supply chain. This can include conducting supplier evaluations, implementing risk management plans and implementing strategies to reduce risk.
